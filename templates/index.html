Hackmas Challenge Rules

  • Eligibility:
    • Open to all skill levels; participants must register through the official form.
    • Only valid registration will be considered for participation.
  • General Rules:
    • Participants must solve challenges independently.
    • Use of publicly available resources is allowed, but sharing solutions with others is not.
    • Malicious or unethical behavior will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Reporting Requirements:
    • A detailed report of the challenge, including steps taken, tools used, and insights gained, is compulsory.
  • Time and Submission:
    • The challenge runs from the 11th to the 20th of December, 2024.
    • Reports and solutions must be submitted before the 20th of December.
  • Winner Selection:
    • Two winners will be selected based on:
      • Creativity and clarity in the submitted report.
      • Accuracy of solutions.
      • Speed of completion.
  • Disqualification:
    • Violating any rules.
    • Sharing solutions during the challenge.
    • Failing to submit the required report.
  • Confidentiality:
    • Participants must not disclose any challenge details or solutions publicly.
